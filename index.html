Who we are

Green Nexus Advisory (GNA) is a Tirana-based advisory and delivery team working across Albania and the Western Balkans. We help partners translate evidence into implementable action—bridging strategy, institutions, and on-the-ground realities.

Our mission

To support a just and resilient transition by strengthening environmental governance, accelerating energy transition planning, and improving local development systems through practical, results-oriented work.

  • Evidence → action: analytics that feeds decisions, budgets, and delivery
  • Institutions matter: roles, coordination, and accountability mechanisms
  • Local focus: municipalities and communities as the delivery engine

How we work

  • Diagnostics: policy & institutional mapping, gaps, risks, opportunities
  • Co-design: stakeholder engagement, facilitation, consensus-building
  • Implementation: roadmaps, action plans, SOPs, capacity building
  • Learning: monitoring, evaluation, and adaptive management

Services offered

Targeted advisory support and end-to-end delivery across governance, energy transition, local systems, and MEL.

Environmental & Climate Governance

  • Policy design and legal / institutional reform support
  • Climate adaptation governance and risk management frameworks
  • Compliance, prevention, and multi-agency coordination support
  • Stakeholder engagement and facilitation

Energy Transition & Energy Security

  • NECP review, gap analysis, and implementation support
  • Renewables policy support and project pipeline structuring
  • Energy efficiency governance and institutional workflows
  • Resilience and disruption risk assessments

Local Development & Delivery Systems

  • Municipal planning and delivery strengthening
  • Natural-capital integration into plans and services
  • Participatory approaches (forums, focus groups, co-design)
  • Capacity-building: training, toolkits, facilitation

Data, tools & decision support

  • Baselines and needs assessments
  • Evidence synthesis (briefs, notes, memos)

Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)

  • Learning loops and adaptive management
